About The Programme

Building Future Technology Professionals

At JKS Soft Tech, we are committed to cultivating a culture of inclusion, collaboration, and continuous learning. Our internship programme provides students with opportunities to develop technical capabilities while gaining experience working within a professional technology environment.

Designed to prepare students for exciting careers in technology, our programmes provide exposure to leading-edge technologies, agile delivery practices, and real-world digital transformation projects.

Your Experience

Learn Through Real-World Projects

Our interns work in an agile environment, contributing to projects that deliver quality software solutions across multiple industry domains. Depending on business requirements and internship objectives, interns gain experience in software development, testing cycles, deployment processes, and professional delivery standards.

Interns also have opportunities to expand their knowledge in emerging technologies such as Java, Tibco, MuleSoft, AWS, digital consulting, design thinking, user experience design, and SaaS/PaaS platforms.

What You Can Expect

Develop Skills Beyond The Classroom

  • Gain first-hand insights into technologies driving multinational companies and startups.
  • Work on high-impact projects covering analysis, design, delivery, and reporting.
  • Develop technical and professional skills through mentorship, outreach activities, and networking sessions.
  • Experience an agile working culture with industry professionals.

Eligibility

Who Can Apply?

Our internship opportunities are open to undergraduate and postgraduate students across academic disciplines, including BE/B.Tech/MCA programmes.

Students are encouraged to commit at least 12 weeks to gain a meaningful internship experience. University students can participate during summer vacations, professional attachment periods, or through our online internship option.
